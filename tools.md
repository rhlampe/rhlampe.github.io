<h2><em>Pseudo-nitzschia</em> Iron Limitation Index (ILI) Calculator</h2>
<img src="/images/psn_ili.png" align="right" width="350" hspace="20" style="box-shadow: 3px 3px 1px #888888; border:3px solid black; margin-bottom:20px">
<a href="https://marchettilab.web.unc.edu/PsnILI/">https://marchettilab.web.unc.edu/PsnILI/</a>
<br /><br />
<p>The <em>Pseudo-nitzschia</em> ILI was developed to assess the in situ iron status of <em>Pseudo-nitzschia</em> with a incubation-independent, molecular-based approach. Here we provide a calculator to rapidly assess the <em>Pseudo-nitzschia</em> ILI from raw RNA-seq counts of two genes: <em>FTN</em> and <em>ISIP2a</em>.</p>
<p>Citation: Marchetti A, Moreno CM, Cohen NR, Oleinikov I, Delong K, Twining BS, Armbrust EV, and Lampe RH. (2017) <a style="color:#0645AD" href="http://dx.doi.org/10.1111/jpy.12539">Development of a molecular-based index for assessing iron status in bloom-forming pennate diatoms</a>. <i>Journal of Phycology</i> 53, 820-832.</p>
